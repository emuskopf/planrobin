Why we built this

Figuring out a Medicare drug plan matters — and it shouldn't be this hard.

It usually starts at a kitchen table. Someone is worried about the cost of their prescriptions — maybe it's you, maybe it's a loved one — the enrollment window is open, but now what?

Did your plan change?

Are your medication costs going up?

Are your prescriptions even covered anymore?

All important questions. PlanRobin exists to give you clear, honest answers — with no salesperson steering you toward a plan that pays them and costs you.

So that's all PlanRobin does: it takes the data Medicare already publishes and shows you the math — what your medications would actually cost across every plan in your county, with the reasoning in plain sight. When federal law caps a price, we show you that too, and say why.

And when the right answer is a human, we'll point you to one who isn't paid to sell you anything: every state runs a free counseling program (SHIP) with trained, unbiased counselors. We link them on every page.

What PlanRobin is — and isn't

Free, and always will be. There is nothing to buy here.
Private. No accounts, no logins, no cookies that follow you. Your medication list is used only to run your search — never stored, never shared, never sold.
Non-commercial. We don't sell insurance, take commissions, run ads, or sell data. No one calls you. Ever.
Independent. A private website — not affiliated with the federal Medicare program or any insurance company.
Educational, not advice. We show estimates built from public data. Always confirm a plan's details on Medicare.gov, at 1-800-MEDICARE, or with a SHIP counselor before enrolling.
Want a human in your corner? Free, unbiased Medicare counseling — no sales pitch — is available from your State Health Insurance Assistance Program (SHIP). Find a counselor → or call 1-800-MEDICARE.
