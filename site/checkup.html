3 Which medications do you take?

Your medication list is never stored. We only send anonymous drug codes to look up prices — never a name, and nothing is saved.

Start typing a drug name — misspellings are fine. Pick the exact strength and form. Brand and generic are shown separately; we never substitute one for the other silently. Add 1–10 medications.
